Since 2011, I have been working at the Benaroya Research Institute where I am currently the Bioinformatics Core Manager. Until 2010, I was the Director of Scientific Computing in the Bioinformatics Program at ZymoGenetics in Seattle, WA.

Before moving to Seattle I worked at UC San Francisco with Fred Cohen as a Visiting Postdoctoral Researcher from April 1988 to April 1989, and then an Assistant Research Chemist to Oct 1993, in the Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry.

I earned a Ph.D. in Chemistry from Harvard Univeristy, fulfilling my residency there, but spending two of of my graduate school years at the ETH in Zurich, Switzerland.
